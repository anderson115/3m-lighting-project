## Solid
**Rating:** 4.0 out of 5 stars
**Author:** Jay P
**Date:** Reviewed in the United States on October 31, 2025
‚úÖ Verified Purchase

Used almost the entire box for a Halloween costume project, primarily to hold EVA foam pieces together. The sheer amount of product is the value here. For the most part it does the job. I did experience some inconsistent performance with more weight-bearing applications, both with the hook and loop as well as with the adhesive. I won't be pulling apart the attached pieces apart often, if at all, but I would have reasonable concerns about the long-term sticking power (hook and loop - and - adhesive) if I did. I've used other brands before that I trusted that are no longer available, and while I would buy this item again, my level of confidence isn't quite as high. But, for semi-permanent usage with minimal weight-bearing, it's solid.

## If you're hanging art? These probably aren't for you.
**Rating:** 3.0 out of 5 stars
**Author:** H. A.
**Date:** Reviewed in the United States on October 11, 2025
‚úÖ Verified Purchase

If you're like me and have a ton of art to hang, you might be looking for a Command Strip alternative that isn't as expensive. Unfortunately, these don't quite fit the bill.

They might be great for mounting other things, or securing carpets etc, but if you're trying to hang frames (light ones, even!), these will not be a suitable alternative to Command Strips for the vast majority of what you need to hang.

Pros:
Very sticky.
Don't seem to damage walls unless the paint is already kind of sketchy, which is a big deal!

Unfortunately, these just didn't serve my needs, and despite lots of reviews, I really had to post this one. I've had several pieces of art just fall off the wall out of nowhere, and that's really frustrating. No breakage yet, but I'm not going to be waiting to see if that happens. I'll just be spending the money on big Command Strip.
