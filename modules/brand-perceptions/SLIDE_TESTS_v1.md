### SO WHAT? (Strategic Implications)

**THREE KEY INSIGHTS:**

**1. Specialization is EMERGING but not COMPLETE:**
- Each brand has clear territory (renter, DIY, heavy-duty)
- But 76% of content overlaps in picture hanging
- Need stronger differentiation to avoid cannibalization

**2. Market GAPS represent growth opportunity:**
- Budget segment unaddressed (vulnerability to generics)
- Outdoor/extreme applications limited by temperature
- Kitchen/bathroom underserved (6% of content vs 30%+ of home)
- Light-duty adhesive gap (between "premium" and "serious weight")

**3. Content distribution MISMATCHES market size:**
- Renters = 36% of households, but only 18% of content
- DIY market growing 35% YoY, but only 46% of videos (89/193)
- Kitchen/bathroom = 30% of home, but only 6% of content
