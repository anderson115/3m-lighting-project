 Cheap and easy garage organizer start out by cleaning your workspace and marking where your studs are Next take your selected piece of wood. I use this xx by 2 and plan out how you want your tools to hang Then go to Harbor Freight pick up this hook set for 599 plan mark and drill where you want your hook holes to go Sand aboard using 120 grit paint or stain how you want your boards to look. I use black for feature aesthetic Go ahead and screw in your hooks When hanging the board make sure it's leveled drill pilot holes and then screw into where the studs are Go ahead and hang tools and that is a job well done
