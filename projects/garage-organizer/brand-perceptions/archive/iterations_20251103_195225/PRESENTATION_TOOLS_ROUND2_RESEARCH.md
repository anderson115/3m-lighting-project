### ‚ö†Ô∏è MAJOR FINDING - Gamma Export Issues

**Round 1 Status:** Not in top 3 (disqualified for no PPTX export)
**Round 2 Revelation:** Gamma DOES export to PPTX, BUT with SEVERE fidelity issues
