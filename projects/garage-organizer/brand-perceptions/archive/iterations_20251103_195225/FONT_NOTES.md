- Slightly condensed
- Corporate heritage (IBM)
- Distinctive character

### Vibe
"IBM/Financial Services/Data-Driven"

## ðŸ”¤ **3. Work Sans**

### Characteristics
- Geometric, friendly
- Slightly rounded
- Contemporary
- Approachable

### Vibe
"Mailchimp/Slack/Modern Friendly"

- Friendly without being casual
- Geometric but not cold

- Bold, impactful

- Modern, assertive
- High x-height

"Bold Startup/Modern Agency"

- Modern agencies
- When you want maximum presence
